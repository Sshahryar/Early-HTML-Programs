Consequences of Copyrights

There are Conseqeunces in Canada Regarding Copyright Violations

  • The violator will pay the amount of damages the violation caused, this typically goes from $750 to $30, 000 per copyright infringement.
  • The violator may also face imprisonment up to 2 years.
  • The violator can also be sued and get a federal criminal record.
